What is this?

Retro Game Sprite Comic Hour is a "Comic Strip" in the "Sprite Comics" genre intended to parody old "Comic Strip(s)" in the same genre. It takes inspiration from sprite comics occasionally posted by Tony "Pressed Yes" and Megaman Sprite Comic.

I think old sprite comics are funny.
